# Brownian Motion

A standard Brownian motion $W_t$ satisfies $W_0 = 0$, independent increments, and
$W_t - W_s \sim \mathcal{N}(0, t-s)$.

Key properties:
- Quadratic variation: $[W]_t = t$
- Not differentiable almost everywhere
